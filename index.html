Get started
Up and running
in four steps
1

Check eligibility

The app is available to residents in buildings where the cosy.green system is already installed by your landlord.

2

Download the app

Get cosy.home from the App Store or Google Play and sign up with the credentials provided by your building manager.

3

Explore your rooms

See all your thermostats, current temperatures, and live humidity data for every room in your apartment.

4

Create your first schedule

Set a weekly heating plan for your whole apartment or per room — the system handles the rest automatically.
