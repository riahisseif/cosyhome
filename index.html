Every feature is designed to make your home's heating feel effortless — not another thing to manage.

01
🌡️

Live Thermostat Status

Real-time temperature and humidity from every thermostat in your apartment, refreshed continuously.

02

Smart Schedules

Set heating plans for the whole apartment or per room. The system delivers your target temperature at exactly the right moment.

03
🏠

Room-by-Room Control

Warm the bedroom before you wake up, cool the office in the afternoon. Each room runs on its own schedule.

Get started
Up and running
in four steps
1

Check eligibility

The app is available to residents in buildings where the cosy.green system is installed by your landlord.

2

Download the app

Get cosy.home from the App Store or Google Play and sign in with credentials from your building manager.

3

Explore your rooms

See all your thermostats, live temperatures and humidity readings for every room in your apartment.

4

Create a schedule

Set a weekly heating plan for your whole apartment or per room — the system handles the rest automatically.
